About Me

Hi, I’m Akowuah Christian Sarpong, and my journey began with the simple act of planting a seed beside my grandmother. I remember the smell of the soil, the warmth of the sun, and her quiet reminder that caring for the earth means caring for life itself. That moment shaped the way I see the world — as something fragile, interconnected, and worth protecting. Today, I’m drawn to environmental science because I want to understand how our actions affect the planet and how technology can help us make things right. From exploring data that tells nature’s story to designing small solutions that make sustainability easier, I’m learning how science and compassion can work together. For me, this path isn’t just about studying the environment — it’s about honoring it, using what I know to help it heal, and inspiring others to do the same.
