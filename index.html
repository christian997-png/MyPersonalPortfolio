Environmental Science and Technology Integration Having a strong foundation in environmental science, I am passionate about integrating technology with sustainable practices. Problem-Solving and Innovation I thrive on solving complex problems, whether that means debugging code, optimizing network performance, or innovating new ways to reduce a system's environmental impact. My projects are rooted in a commitment to both technical excellence and a broader vision of using technology to improve lives and sustain the planet. With my interdisciplinary knowledge in technology and environmental science, I aim to develop solutions that are both innovative and sustainable, making a positive impact on businesses and communities alike.
